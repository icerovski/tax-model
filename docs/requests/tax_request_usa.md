# Request for Professional Tax Advice: USA
**Project:** Wyoming or Delaware Single-Member LLC (Disregarded Entity)
**Profile:** Non-US Citizen, Bulgarian Resident (Non-Resident Alien for US Tax).

---

## 1. Background
My intention is to incorporate a Single-Member LLC in Wyoming or Delaware. The LLC will provide consulting services to European clients and hold investment accounts (Trading/Dividends). I will perform all services remotely from Bulgaria and have no physical presence (employees, office, inventory) in the USA initially. However, later on, the LLC might seek to invest directly in a business in the USA.

**The Specific Consulting Service:** The LLC will be contracted by a European client to provide specialized market intelligence and research on the **US PV/BESS (Photovoltaic / Battery Energy Storage Systems) market**. All actual analytical work will be performed by me while physically located in Bulgaria.

## 2. Scope of Inquiry: Federal Income Tax (Non-ETBUS Status)
*   **Premise A:** Based on the absence of a US "Agent" or fixed place of business, the LLC is not **Engaged in Trade or Business in the US (ETBUS)**. Furthermore, providing US-centric market research does not create a US nexus if the work is executed entirely outside the US. Therefore, consulting income is 0% taxable in the USA. Please confirm.
*   **Premise B:** Confirm that investment income (Capital Gains from stock trading) is not effectively connected income (ECI) and is not taxable in the US for my profile.
*   **Premise C:** Please advise if my future plans to invest in a business in the USA would change my ETBUS status and what structural precautions I should take.

## 3. Scope of Inquiry: Reporting Obligations
As a Foreign-Owned Disregarded Entity, the LLC has specific filing requirements.
*   **Requirement A:** Confirm the requirement to file **Form 5472** and **Form 1120 (pro-forma)** annually. What are the current penalties for non-compliance in 2026?
*   **Requirement B:** Confirm if an **EIN** is sufficient or if I need an **ITIN**, given there is no US tax liability currently.

## 4. Scope of Inquiry: Treaty Application
*   **Dividends:** I receive US-sourced dividends via Interactive Brokers. Confirm that the **US-Bulgaria Tax Treaty** limits the withholding tax to 15% (or 5% if specific ownership thresholds are met).
*   **W-8BEN-E:** Confirm that the LLC should file Form W-8BEN-E with the broker to claim treaty benefits as a disregarded entity owned by a BG resident.

## 5. Deliverables
A confirmation of the tax-neutral status of the LLC for this specific operational model and a checklist of required annual filings to maintain compliance.
