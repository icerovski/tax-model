# Request for Professional Tax & Regulatory Advice: USA
**Project:** Wyoming or Delaware Single-Member LLC (Disregarded Entity)
**Profile:** Non-US Citizen, Bulgarian Resident (Non-Resident Alien for US Tax).

---

## 1. Background
My intention is to incorporate a Single-Member LLC in Wyoming or Delaware. The LLC will provide consulting services to a European client and hold investment accounts (Trading/Dividends) funded by the profits from that consulting work. I will perform all services remotely from Bulgaria and have no physical presence in the USA.

**The Specific Consulting Service:** The LLC will contract with a European client to provide market research on the **US PV/BESS (Photovoltaic / Battery Energy Storage Systems) sector** and help traders facilitate **physical commercial contracts** (e.g., PPAs or BESS Tolling agreements) with asset owners. These are commodity/service agreements, not the acquisition of shares, equity, or SPVs.

**Fee Structure:** The LLC will be compensated via a **fixed consulting retainer** or project-based fee, not transaction-based commissions.

## 2. Scope of Inquiry: Regulatory Compliance (Advisers Act & 1934 Act)
I require a combined analysis of the regulatory and tax implications of this model.
*   **Investment Advice:** Confirm if providing impersonal US-centric market research reports qualifies for the "Publisher's Exclusion" under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940.
*   **Broker-Dealer Status:** Given that I am facilitating physical commercial contracts for a fixed fee (not commissions), confirm that the LLC is not "effecting transactions in securities" and thus does not trigger Broker-Dealer registration requirements.
*   **Nexus Impact:** Please assess if any aspect of these activities would force the LLC to register as a US Broker or Adviser, thereby creating a "US Regulatory Nexus."

## 3. Scope of Inquiry: Federal Income Tax (Non-ETBUS Status)
*   **ETBUS Status:** Confirm that the LLC is not **Engaged in Trade or Business in the US (ETBUS)** based on the absence of a US office or dependent agents.
*   **Regulatory Link:** Specifically, confirm that avoiding US regulatory registration is critical to maintaining this Non-ETBUS status (and the 0% US tax rate).
*   **Investment Income:** Confirm that capital gains from trading are not taxable in the US for this profile.

## 4. Scope of Inquiry: Reporting Obligations & Treaty
*   **Reporting:** Confirm annual filing requirements (Form 5472, Pro-forma 1120).
*   **Treaty:** Confirm that the US-Bulgaria Tax Treaty limits withholding on US dividends to 15%.
*   **W-8BEN-E:** Confirm the LLC's eligibility to claim treaty benefits as a disregarded entity.

## 5. Deliverables
A combined memorandum addressing both the tax-neutral status and the regulatory safe-harbors for this specific operational model.
