# LLC Annual Maintenance: Quote Request Drafts

This document contains two versions of an email to circulate to corporate service providers (Corpag, W&G, etc.) to ensure a **predictable, flat-fee** annual cost structure for your US LLC.

---

## Version 1: High-Level (Recommended for initial outreach)

**Subject:** Quote Request: All-inclusive Flat Annual Fee for [Proposed LLC Name]

Hi [Name],

I am currently evaluating providers for the incorporation and ongoing management of my new [Wyoming/Delaware] LLC. 

As the entity will be active with consulting income and proprietary trading, I am looking for a **single, all-inclusive annual fee** that covers all mandatory US compliance and corporate governance. I need to avoid hourly billing or percentage-based fees. 

Could you please provide a fixed annual quote for a "keep-well" bundle that includes:

1.  **Statutory Maintenance:** Registered Agent fees and State Annual Report filings.
2.  **Federal Compliance:** Preparation and filing of **Form 5472** and the **pro-forma Form 1120**.
3.  **Regulatory Reporting:** Handling the initial and any recurring BOI (FinCEN) filings.
4.  **Corporate Governance:** Providing a US-based Professional Manager/Nominee to represent the company and sign contracts (POEM mitigation).

Please provide one flat fee for this complete service package. I am looking for full cost predictability to ensure the entity remains in good standing without unexpected overhead.

Best regards,

Ivan Cerovski

## Internal PE Review Note (for Ivan)
*   **Target All-In Budget:** For Wyoming, a professional package (including a Nominee Manager and Tax Prep) should ideally fall between **$2,500 – $4,500/year** as a flat fee. 
*   **Wyoming vs. Delaware:** Wyoming remains the more predictable choice, as the state fee is a fixed $60 regardless of your assets. Delaware's tax can increase if your corporate balance sheet grows significantly.
*   **POEM Mitigation (The Manager):** This is your most important defense. By having a US-based manager who actually signs the consulting contracts with your European traders, you can prove to the Bulgarian NRA that the "Effective Management" happens outside of Sofia.
*   **The 5472 Guarantee:** Ensure the provider guarantees the filing of Form 5472 within their flat fee. This form is the most common reason for IRS penalties ($25,000+).
