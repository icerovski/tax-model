Gross Dividends (€): Total dividends received.

Need to know: Are these from US stocks? (US stocks trigger an automatic 15% withholding at Interactive Brokers, which we must credit against your Bulgarian 5% tax).

Trading Profits (€): Net realized capital gains.

Need to know: Are you trading on EU-regulated markets (0% Bulgarian tax) or US/International markets (10% Bulgarian tax)?

2. The Expense Inputs
LLC Operating Costs (€): Your annual costs for the Wyoming setup (Registered Agent, US CPA for Form 5472, software, etc.).

Why this matters: Bulgaria gives you a flat 25% statutory deduction for consulting, so your actual expenses don't change your tax bill, but they do change your true net wealth.

3. The "MD Shield" Inputs
Your Monthly MD Salary (€): * Why this matters: The 2026 Bulgarian social security cap is €2,111.64. If your PE salary exceeds this, the script will automatically zero out any social security on your Wyoming income.

Client Withholding (True/False): Is your Bulgarian client withholding the 10% Technical Services Tax at the source? (This calculates your year-end "cash out of pocket" vs. tax already paid).

def calculate_2026_wealth(
    gross_consulting: float,
    gross_dividends: float,
    gross_trading: float,
    monthly_md_salary: float,
    llc_annual_expenses: float,
    is_us_dividend: bool = True,
    is_eu_trading: bool = False,
    client_withholds_tax: bool = True
):
    # 2026 Bulgarian Tax Constants (Euro adopted)
    BG_INCOME_TAX = 0.10
    BG_DIVIDEND_TAX = 0.05
    STATUTORY_DEDUCTION = 0.25
    MAX_SOC_SEC_CAP = 2111.64
    SOC_SEC_RATE = 0.278 # Approx total rate for self-insured

    print("--- 2026 WYOMING LLC & BULGARIAN TAX PROJECTION ---")

    # 1. Social Security Check (The "MD Shield")
    if monthly_md_salary >= MAX_SOC_SEC_CAP:
        soc_sec_due = 0.0
        print("[+] Social Security: €0.00 (Max cap reached via MD salary)")
    else:
        # Simplified calculation for the gap
        gap = MAX_SOC_SEC_CAP - monthly_md_salary
        soc_sec_due = gap * 12 * SOC_SEC_RATE
        print(f"[-] Social Security Due: €{soc_sec_due:.2f}")

    # 2. Consulting Tax
    taxable_consulting = gross_consulting * (1 - STATUTORY_DEDUCTION)
    consulting_tax_liability = taxable_consulting * BG_INCOME_TAX
    
    if client_withholds_tax:
        client_withheld_amount = gross_consulting * BG_INCOME_TAX
        consulting_tax_due_in_april = consulting_tax_liability - client_withheld_amount
        print(f"[-] Consulting Tax Liability: €{consulting_tax_liability:.2f} (Client withheld: €{client_withheld_amount:.2f})")
    else:
        consulting_tax_due_in_april = consulting_tax_liability
        print(f"[-] Consulting Tax Due: €{consulting_tax_liability:.2f}")

    # 3. Dividend Tax & US Treaty Withholding
    if is_us_dividend:
        us_withholding = gross_dividends * 0.15
        bg_dividend_tax_due = 0.0 # US 15% covers the BG 5%
        print(f"[-] Dividend Tax: €{us_withholding:.2f} (Paid to US via 15% Treaty. €0 due in BG)")
    else:
        bg_dividend_tax_due = gross_dividends * BG_DIVIDEND_TAX
        print(f"[-] Dividend Tax Due (BG): €{bg_dividend_tax_due:.2f}")

    # 4. Trading Gains Tax
    if is_eu_trading:
        trading_tax_due = 0.0
        print("[+] Trading Tax: €0.00 (EU/EEA regulated market exemption applied)")
    else:
        # Assuming 10% statutory deduction on financial assets
        trading_tax_due = (gross_trading * 0.90) * BG_INCOME_TAX
        print(f"[-] Trading Tax Due (BG): €{trading_tax_due:.2f}")

    # --- TOTALS ---
    total_tax_paid_or_due = (
        soc_sec_due + 
        consulting_tax_liability + 
        (us_withholding if is_us_dividend else bg_dividend_tax_due) + 
        trading_tax_due
    )
    
    total_gross = gross_consulting + gross_dividends + gross_trading
    true_net_wealth = total_gross - total_tax_paid_or_due - llc_annual_expenses

    print("-" * 50)
    print(f"Total Gross LLC Income:     €{total_gross:,.2f}")
    print(f"Total Taxes (BG + US):      €{total_tax_paid_or_due:,.2f}")
    print(f"LLC Running Expenses:       €{llc_annual_expenses:,.2f}")
    print(f"TRUE NET RETAINED WEALTH:   €{true_net_wealth:,.2f}")
    print(f"Effective Tax Rate:         {(total_tax_paid_or_due / total_gross) * 100:.2f}%\n")

# Run the scenario
